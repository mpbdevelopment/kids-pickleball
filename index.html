What Kids Learn

Our clinics build skills, confidence, and a lifelong love for an amazing sport.

🏓

Fundamentals

Proper grip, stance, serves, volleys, and dinking — all the core skills taught by expert coaches.

🎯

Strategy & Game Play

Court positioning, shot selection, and match play so kids learn to think like athletes.

💪

Fitness & Agility

Hand-eye coordination, footwork drills, and cardio fitness — all disguised as fun!

🤝

Teamwork & Sportsmanship

Doubles play, communication, and respect for opponents builds character on and off the court.

🌟

Confidence

Mastering new skills and competing builds real self-confidence that extends beyond the court.

🎉

Fun & Friendships

A positive, encouraging environment where kids make friends and develop a love for the game.
